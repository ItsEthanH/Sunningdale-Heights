Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Aura GOC and Affiliates

Effective Date: November 1, 2023
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Aura GOC and its affiliates ("we," "our," "us") are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and share information through our social media platforms. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the information we collect on social media and how we manage it.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our social media pages, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, username, profile picture, contact details, and other publicly available profile information.
  • Engagement Data: Comments, likes, shares, mentions, direct messages, and other interactions.
  • Content You Share: Any posts, photos, videos, or other media that you tag us in or share on our pages.
  • Technical Data: IP address, device information, and browsing activity (as collected by social media platforms).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To engage with our audience and respond to inquiries or comments.
  • To improve our social media presence and content strategy.
  • To promote our services, events, and campaigns.
  • To monitor and analyze trends and user behavior on our platforms.
  • To enforce our terms of use and protect against unauthorized or harmful activities.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share information:

  • With social media platforms, as governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • With third-party service providers assisting us in managing our social media presence.
  • If required by law, legal process, or government request.
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety and that of our users.

4. Third-Party Links and Content

Our social media pages may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may update or remove your information from our social media pages by adjusting your privacy settings or deleting posts.
  • You can manage how social media platforms collect and share your data by reviewing their privacy settings.
  • If you wish to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we have collected, you may contact us at info@auragoc.com

6. Data Retention

We retain social media interactions and data as long as necessary for our business purposes or as required by law. You may request removal of specific interactions by contacting us.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on our social media pages and/or website with the updated effective date.
